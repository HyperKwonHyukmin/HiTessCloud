Structural Assessment Menu

AM-Based FEM

1D Beam

1D Beam FEM is to identify structure characteristics of Support, Platform and other 1D Beam structures to predict its stress and displacement with FEM Analsysis. The assessment results will be used to present appropriate counter-measures to avoid excessive stress and displacement.

AM-Based FEM

Truss

Truss analysis automation for LNG carriers enhances safety and efficiency. Used in LNG tank insulation, Truss structures require stability checks under different conditions. Automation streamlines FE modeling, enables quick evaluations during design changes, and stores results for future applications.

AM-Based FEM

PSA

Piping stress analysis is performed in accordance with relevant codes such as ASME B31.3 to ensure that the piping system is structurally safe and functionally sound under various loading conditions, such as internal pressure, temperature changes, support configurations, and external forces
